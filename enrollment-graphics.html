Graduate Student Enrollment per Year

Graduate student enrollment at the Philip Merrill College of Journalism has slipped in recent years, after strong showings in 2012 and 2015. This may reflect collective uncertainty about the future of the industry and an apprehension among students as to the viability of the degree.

Source: Data collected by the University of Maryland, College Park.
